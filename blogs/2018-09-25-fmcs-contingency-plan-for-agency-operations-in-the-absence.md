This FMCS memorandum sets forth a contingency plan that will be operational in the event of a lapse in appropriations to fund current Federal Mediation and Conciliation Service activities. The plan provides for an orderly suspension of agency operations and for the continuation of those agency operations deemed to be excepted from suspension.
